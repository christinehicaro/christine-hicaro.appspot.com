TARGET Intern
Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory (Fermilab)
Batavia, IL (June 24 - August 2, 2013)

TARGET is a 6-week summer engineering program consisting of an internship and afternoon classes. I was 1 of 18 chosen out of 200 applicants to participate in the program. For my internship portion, I helped create a Microsoft SharePoint site for the Benefits Office of Human Resources and also learned elementary physics in relation to the ASTA magnet at the lab from a physicist. Afternoon classes consisted of completing the following engineering-based projects: soldering a radio, building/soldering a power supply, soldering a detector, designing a rocket cap with AutoCAD, creating a 3D object of choice with AutoCAD, building a Newton's cradle, and making/designing a boomerang.
